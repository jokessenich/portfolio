bio

I am a lifelong learner and explorer, enrolled in the Thinkful Full Stack Development Program. I enjoy coding because understanding the mechanisms behind the world that I interact with fascinates me. Building code from the ground up is an immensely challenging and exciting task with a humbling sense of accomplishment. I love to embrace the challenges I receive and to learn from them all I can.

By night I love to have adventures and find joy in the natural world around me. You can find me coated in sand from playing beach volleyball, living off the grid in a cabin that was built with friends, traveling to a new place that my heart has called me to, or making a cup of tea for my loving partner.

Seinfeld Quiz App

See the App
See the Repo

This Seinfeld Quiz App is my first design of an app from scratch with html, CSS and JavaScript.

The code is semantically clear and user accessible. The quiz consists of a start page, ten question pages, a page verifying whether your answer was correct, and an overall result page.

The score and question number along with the Seinfeld logo are kept neatly. This project also includes unique Seinfeld art work and interesting questions.
